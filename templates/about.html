About


This is an initial version of the PE website, developed by Tamar Michelson and Yuli Tshuva YoloLab at the YOLO Lab.


Our online calculator provides a prediction model to assess the risk of developing preeclampsia (PE) in twin pregnancies, based on clinical parameters available during different trimesters.

This tool utilizes the most important predictive features from the first, second, and third trimesters to calculate the absolute risk of developing PE (expressed as a percentage).

Designed to support physicians throughout the course of a twin pregnancy, our calculator aids in making informed decisions about monitoring, early interventions, and management strategies to mitigate the risks associated with preeclampsia in twin pregnancies.
